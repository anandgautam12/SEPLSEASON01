League Structure

• The league will consist of 32 matches.
• Table topper goes directly into Final.
• 2nd & 3rd place teams play Qualifier.
• Winner of Qualifier faces Topper in Final.

Auction Details

• Base Price: ₹300
• ₹300–700: +₹50 increment
• Above ₹700: +₹100 increment
• Unsold Players → Re-auction

Match Rules

•Overs per Match: 8 overs
•Maximum Bowlers: 4 (A bowler can bowl a maximum of 2 overs)
• Max 2 Overs per Bowler
•Bowling Style: Underarm (bowler's hand must rotate) or Round Arm.
• Powerplay: Total of 3 overs.
• The first 2 overs are compulsory powerplay.
• The batting team can choose 1 over of powerplay between the 6th and 8th over.
• The Umpire's decision will be final.
• LBW (Leg Before Wicket) dismissals will not be given.
• All other standard rules of cricket will remain in effect.
• DRS not available

Trial Match Rules

• Batsman: 2 Overs
• Bowler: 2 Overs
• All-rounder: 1 Over Bat + 1 Over Bowl
